Design Philosophy

  • Embrace simplicity, and amplify nature's beauty. Merge functionality with elegance, weaving vibrant visuals that reflect the commitment to quality, health, and the joy of exceptional tea experiences.

Missions & Target Audience

  • Helping tea shops bridge the gap between their unique products and their brand identity. Aiming to highlight their commitment to natural, low-calorie ingredients by revamping their website to prominently showcase their offerings. Through vibrant visuals and intuitive design, I will enhance brand recognition and create a memorable experience for customers.

  • Health-conscious individuals seeking natural and low-calorie beverage options.

  • Millennials and Gen Z interested in unique and visually appealing products.

  • Online shoppers looking for convenient and visually engaging websites for purchasing beverages.

  • Eco-conscious consumers supporting businesses committed to sustainable practices.

  • Urban professionals seeking refreshing and healthy alternatives during their busy days.

Brand Values

  • Authenticity and Transparency: Emphasizing the use of authentic, transparent ingredients, reinforcing the brand's commitment to honesty and quality.

  • Urban Harmony: Positioning the brand as a harmonious blend of urban sophistication and natural simplicity, catering to the diverse aspects of modern living.

  • Crafted Wellness: Focusing on a holistic approach to wellness, emphasizing not just health but the artistry involved in crafting each drink.

  • Personalized Experience: Offering a personalized and bespoke experience, tailoring beverages to individual tastes, and fostering a deep connection with customers.

Font Pairing

  • Artistic Serif Fonts: Choosing serif fonts with artistic flair, incorporating unique details to convey a sense of creativity and craftsmanship.

  • Katibeh: Title, Navigation

  • Abhaya Libre: Text

Brand Character

  • Innovation: Willing to challenge conventions, pursuing excellence in every beverage, and daring to create an unparalleled taste experience for customers.

  • Natural Elegance: Blending natural elements with refined elegance, showcasing the brand's sophistication and delicacy in the modern beverage landscape.

  • Connection: Striving to cultivate an image full of genuine emotions, building deep emotional connections with customers.

Colours

  • Subdued Earth Tones: Utilizing warm earthy tones like terracotta, olive green, and muted blush to evoke a grounded and organic ambiance.

  • Neutral Base with a Hint of Gold: Incorporating a neutral base color with subtle gold accents to add a touch of sophistication and luxury.
